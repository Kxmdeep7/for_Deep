I didn’t want to say too much. Some feelings lose their meaning when they’re rushed.
So this is written slowly, the way old letters were meant to be read.
Not everything meaningful needs to be explained. Some things are understood quietly.
You were never loud in my life. Yet your presence changed the way silence felt.
Even now, that quiet remains. Not heavy. Not demanding. Just there.
This isn’t about holding on. And it isn’t about asking for anything.
It’s simply about being honest without making things complicated.
Some connections don’t need names. They just need respect.
